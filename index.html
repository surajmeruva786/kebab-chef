Our Story

Founded in 2019, RisottoRaj was born from a passion for bringing together the aromatic spices of Indian cuisine with the comforting dishes of Italian tradition.

Our chefs combine traditional cooking methods from both cultures, creating unique flavors that celebrate the rich heritage of these beloved cuisines.

Set in a casual, nature-inspired atmosphere, our restaurant features abundant greenery, natural materials, and an inviting ambiance that makes every meal feel like a special occasion.
